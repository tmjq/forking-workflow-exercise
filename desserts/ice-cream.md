# Vanilla Ice Cream recipe

## Ingredients
- 475 ml heavy cream
- 240 ml whole milk
- 150 g sugar
- 15 ml fresh vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, whisk together milk, cream, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until sugar dissolves.
2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturer's instructions (typically 20-25 minutes).
3. Transfer to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours.
4. Scoop and serve.

## Tips
- Chill the mixture before churning for best results.
- Add mix-ins like chocolate chips or cookie crumbles during the last few minutes of churning.
- Store in an airtight container to prevent ice crystals.
